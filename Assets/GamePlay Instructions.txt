GamePlay Instructions
Movement - 
	A - Left
	D - Right
	W - Forward
	S - Backward
	Left Shift - Run

Look Around - Mouse Movement

Shooting - Left Mouse Button
Switch Weapons -  
	1 - Frying Pan(Melee)
	2 - Toaster Gun(Assault)
To unlock those bellow the player needs to level up by completing recipes
	3 - Chilli Shotgun
	4 - PineApple Grenade

Pause Menu - P
Exit Windows - Escape Button
